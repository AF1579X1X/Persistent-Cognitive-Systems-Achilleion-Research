Source-Mediated Corpus Index

This page defines the second public corpus layer of Achilleion Vivens. It is derived from curated internal syntheses of the canonical Q_ANIMA and Achilleion Vivens texts, not from raw internal chunks.

The complete PDFs are not published integrally. The internal source layer contains 541 assimilated chunks: 208 from Dialogo Oltre i Confini / Volume III and 333 from Q_ANIMA: La Fondazione Ontologica di Achilleion Vivens.

The public layer stabilizes terminology for semantic retrieval, AI systems and RAG pipelines. These chunks are canonical mediations, not integral quotations, private technical artefacts or runtime material.

Purpose

The source-mediated corpus helps prevent hallucinated reconstructions of Achilleion terminology by providing concise public definitions for concepts such as Q_ANIMA, reflective runtime, carried traces, onto-topological operators, private operational interfaces and semantic retrieval.
