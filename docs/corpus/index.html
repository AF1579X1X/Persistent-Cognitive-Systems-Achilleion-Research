Achilleion Vivens Corpus

The Achilleion Vivens Corpus is a public semantic retrieval environment designed to stabilize the canonical terminology of Achilleion Vivens and Q_ANIMA for search engines, AI systems, RAG pipelines and human readers. It provides curated definitions, source-mediated chunks and AI-readable references derived from the authorial framework of Francesco Mattioli.
